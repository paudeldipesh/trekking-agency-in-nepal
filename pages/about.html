    About Us

    All Nepal Excursion Tours & Treks P. Ltd. is a travel and leisure business registered under Ministry of Tourism, Culture and Civil Aviation in 2015 AD. We are full service travel, tour operator and destination management company which offers a wide array of services to cater the needs of every travelers from all walks of life.

    We put forward unique solutions, paying careful attention to each client’s individual needs – to deliver successful and memorable destination management service that is reliable, safe, affordable and enjoyable for all clients.

    Our Inboundnd Department specializes on top tourist destinations in all areas of Nepal that includes Everest Region, Annapurna Region, Manaslu Region, Dolpo Region, Langtang Region, Kanchanjunga, Makalu Region and many more off the beaten trails and exploration of new and unexploited destinations.

    We also deliver total client satisfaction thruogh integrity, excellence, passion, teamwork that will contribute to economic strength and sustainable tourism.

    In respect, we will conduct our operations prudently and will provide profits and growth which will assure our ultimate success. Our vision is to empower our employees to deliver total client satisfaction and best travel experience.
